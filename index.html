About Me

  • I have always had an interest in working with computers, whether it be learning about various parts of a computer or the code used to make one run. I began my official journey in learning to code in middle school and have been coding ever since.

  • Currently, I am using my experience to work through a bootcamp, 100Devs. Here, I work alongside Web Developers from around the world, many of which have only just begun their journeys.

  • In the future, I would love to work alongside Web Developers with various backgrounds and experiences to shape a world where technology can assist those who need it most.
